About the Club

Welcome to the Innovation & Tech Club! Our mission is to foster creativity, learning, and collaboration among students passionate about technology and innovation.

We host regular workshops, hackathons, and speaker sessions with industry professionals to enhance members' skills and exposure to real-world applications.

The club also encourages peer learning, project development, and community outreach through tech-driven initiatives.

Timeline & History

2018 - Club Founded

The beginning of a passionate community for tech enthusiasts.

2019 - First Hackathon

Organized a 24-hour coding challenge with 100+ participants.

2021 - Partnered with Tech Companies

Collaborated with local startups and tech firms for workshops and internships.

2023 - Community Outreach

Launched free coding sessions for underprivileged students in the region.
